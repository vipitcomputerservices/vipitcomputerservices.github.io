Purpose

This page confirms that a formal DPA is available for enterprise and regulated clients. The signed DPA governs processing of personal data where we act as a processor/service provider on behalf of a client.

Typical DPA Coverage

  • Roles (controller/processor), processing instructions, and scope
  • Security controls and confidentiality obligations
  • Subprocessor terms and transfer safeguards
  • Data subject request support
  • Incident notification and cooperation
  • Deletion/return of data at end of services
  • Audit rights (reasonable and proportionate)
  • International transfer mechanisms (including SCCs where applicable)
