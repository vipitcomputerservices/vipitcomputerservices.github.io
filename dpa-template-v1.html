Data Processing Addendum (DPA) – Template v1

Template for enterprise engagements (non-binding until signed)

Provider: 1001501372 Ontario Inc. (VIP AI & Computer Solutions)

1. Roles and Scope

Client acts as Controller/Business. Provider acts as Processor/Service Provider for personal data processed to deliver contracted services.

2. Processing Details

  • Subject matter: AI setup, automation, support, and related technical services.
  • Duration: Term of services plus limited retention period for legal/operational obligations.
  • Data categories: business contact data, operational metadata, user-provided content.
  • Data subjects: client staff, customers, contractors, and authorized users.

3. Processor Obligations

  • Process personal data only on documented client instructions.
  • Ensure confidentiality obligations for authorized personnel.
  • Implement appropriate technical and organizational safeguards.
  • Assist client with data-subject rights requests where reasonably required.

4. Subprocessors

Provider may use subprocessors for hosting, communications, analytics, and operations support. Provider remains responsible for subprocessor compliance with materially similar data-protection obligations.

5. Cross-Border Transfers

Where required, international transfers are governed by lawful transfer mechanisms, including Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) or equivalent safeguards.

6. Incident Notification

Provider will notify client of confirmed personal data incidents without undue delay and provide available information reasonably needed for client response obligations.

7. Audit and Information Rights

Upon reasonable request, provider will make available information necessary to demonstrate compliance, subject to confidentiality and proportionality limits.

8. Deletion or Return

Upon termination, provider will delete or return personal data as agreed, except where retention is required by law or legitimate record-keeping obligations.

9. Conflict

If this DPA conflicts with the master service terms, this DPA governs solely for data protection matters.
